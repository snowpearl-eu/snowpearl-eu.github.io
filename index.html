What you get with us


Being Digital Nomads ourselves we had this idea of building the ultimate Nomad package, something that includes everything one could possibly need, so we stuck our heads together and here we are.
Snowpearl offers you your own apartment, fully furnished, a coworking space, Sauna, Jacuzzi and Table Tennis, but most of all we'll answer all your questions and get you settled in, after all people are supposed to have fun!

All our social events are open and free for everyone, whether you are an active member or a friend of a friend. There is no charge or membership fee for socializing, making friends and networking.


What else will await you?


If you come here in winter: Skiing, Snowboarding, Snowshoeing, Paragliding, Apres-Ski, Hot Springs
If you come here in summer: Hiking in Pirin National Park, Mountain biking, Hot Springs, a weekend at the beach in Greece ;)

But that ain't all! Thinking about moving here for residency? We'll help you with all the legal paperwork. Need a company? We'll get you set up with our personally approved lawyers and accountants.

Next step: Book your flight!
